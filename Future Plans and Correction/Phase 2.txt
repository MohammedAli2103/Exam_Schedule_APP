# One recommendation

Don't think of Phase 2 as "adding login." Think of it as **making the app multi-user**. Every feature from this point onward‚Äîincluding maintenance mode, quiz generation, analytics, study streaks, and your future AI features‚Äîshould assume that all data belongs to a specific authenticated user. That mindset will help you design the database and Flutter code cleanly from the start.
