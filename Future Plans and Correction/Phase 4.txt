## One recommendation before starting

**Finish Phase 2 (Authentication & Personalization) first.**

Once each user has their own account:

* Their uploaded PDFs belong only to them.
* Generated quizzes are private.
* Quiz history and analytics become user-specific.
* Your Python backend simply processes requests for authenticated users.
